Preeti believes that as a designer she has the unique privilege and responsibility to envision the world at its best. She believes in the power of an authentic story and that it can have a direct impact on how people interact with the brand. She is always finding ways to learn, teach and practice her craft with uncompromising attention to detail and with a love for finding beauty and functionality in everything she designs.

An Atlanta based multi-disciplinary designer with over 22 years of creative experience, Preeti works with companies and agencies of all sizes. Her collaboration with various agencies has led her to work on several notable brands where she has designed and implemented campaigns, brand identity, advertising, packaging, infographics, and other brand touch points. She has created digital and print solutions for notable clients including American Express, AT&T, Bank of America, Chick-fil-A, Comcast, Coca-Cola, Delta Airlines, Equifax, Hospital Corporation of America, SagesGov, and The Home Depot. Over the years she has demonstrated the ability to adapt to different agency cultures and team structures making her an ideal partner in any team.

She graduated from The Portfolio Center, she began her 20 year career as an educator and mentoring students. She currently teaches design and typography at the Miami Ad School.

Prior to moving to Atlanta, Preeti worked at Seigel Gale, New York and in India as a designer and an account executive.
